Sanganer

Sanganer is a busy suburban hub of craft workshops, where local artisans sell traditional Bagru textile prints and handmade paper. Nearby Chokhi Dhani is a replica royal village, drawing crowds for live Rajasthani music and dance, with restaurants serving classic North Indian cuisine. Colorful Jain shrines and temples include the ancient, red-stone Shri Digamber.

Address : Sanganer is a town/ Tehsil (an administrative division) situated in Jaipur district, Rajasthan, 16 km south of state capital Jaipur
